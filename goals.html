Future Goals

Right now, my biggest goal is to continue growing into a well-rounded professional who understands both business needs and technical systems. I know I am still early in my career, so my future goals are focused on growth, experience, and building a strong foundation.

Goal 1: Gain Strong Internship Experience

I want to earn internship opportunities where I can apply what I am learning in class to real business problems. I am especially interested in roles related to information systems, analytics, operations, and business technology.

Goal 2: Keep Improving My Technical Skills

I want to keep improving in SQL, Python, and other tools that help turn data into decisions. I also want to become better at using technology not just correctly, but thoughtfully and professionally.

Goal 3: Grow as a Communicator

It is important to me that I can explain my ideas clearly. I want to continue improving the way I write, present, and communicate so that I can contribute well in both team settings and professional settings.

Longer-Term Vision

Longer term, I want to build a career where I can solve meaningful problems, continue learning, and contribute to organizations in a reliable way. I want to be known as someone who is thoughtful, dependable, and capable of handling both technical details and broader goals.

I also want to stay open-minded. As I gain more experience, I know my interests may become more specific, but I want to keep building the kind of foundation that gives me options.

Why These Goals Matter to Me

These goals matter because I do not just want a job. I want to grow into someone who adds value, keeps improving, and takes pride in doing work well. That may sound simple, but it matters to me, and I think it is a good direction to keep moving toward.
