About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Management Information Systems at San Diego State University while minoring in Data Science. My academic interests are centered around data, systems, technology in business, and the way well-designed processes can make organizations run better.

I am especially interested in work where I can think analytically, communicate clearly, and help improve the way people and systems interact. I like learning how business needs translate into technical solutions, and I like being in roles where both detail and professionalism matter.

My Background

My work experience has given me exposure to customer service, operations, maintenance, teamwork, and accountability. At TownePlace Marriott, I worked in both breakfast operations and front desk support, which taught me how to stay organized in fast-paced environments while keeping service quality high. In my current role with Associated Students, I help maintain fitness equipment and support facility operations in a setting where consistency, safety, and attention to detail matter every day.

These experiences have helped me become more dependable and more comfortable solving problems in real time. They also made me appreciate the value of efficient systems, because even small improvements can make a noticeable difference in daily operations.

My Professional Interests

I am interested in analytics, business technology, process improvement, and operational problem-solving. I enjoy looking at how information is tracked, how processes flow, and how teams can make better decisions through data and better systems.

I am also interested in roles where I can keep learning. As a student, I know I am still building my experience, but I also know that I am someone who is willing to learn, willing to improve, and willing to take feedback seriously. That matters to me a lot.
